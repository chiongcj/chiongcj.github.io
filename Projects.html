SafeSpace

A mobile application designed to proivde students with a platform engage and interaction with different resouces catered towards improving overall mental health. Safe SafeSpace encourages students to document their daily experiences, thoughts, and reflections through daily journal entries. Tailored to meet the unique needs of the academic community, it provides various features such as customizable templates, privacy settings, and mood tracking. This app aims to support students' mental well-being and personal growth by facilitating a habit of reflective writing.
