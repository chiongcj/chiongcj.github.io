ZotSquat


Problem Statement

The ARC (Anteater Recreation Center) is often overcrowded
and the lines prevent people from being able to utilize
their time properly. This can make the ARC become less
and less appealing when they see every machine is
practically in use. Considering the ARC helps with students
social life and overall health, we want UCI students to easily
be able to access the ARC and develop their personal schedules
around peak times

Research & Analysis

Research methods included using google forms and
interviews with the target demographic of UCI students

Came to the two key points that:

Majority of students spend a short amount of time at the gym

Waiting for machines has a major impact on productivity at the gym

Personas

Developed two user personas catering towards students
experiencing the arc to determine features and problems
we want to incorporate and resolve

Prototype

Developed Hi-Fidelity prototypes of the app
